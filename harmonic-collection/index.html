ABOUT THE COLLECTION

For my harmonic collection I wanted to continue doing more research into art history. Although I am enrolled in multiple art history courses, there is always so much more to learn. I have found that a majority of my peers, being that we are completing digital work, are not interested in the history of our majors. However, that does not include all of the communication design majors. Many are invested in learning more about each art movement and how it took form. That is why I wanted to form my code around a theme that I could also learn from.
